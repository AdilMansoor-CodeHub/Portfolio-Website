Zombie Showdown: Hyper-Casual Game Development

Overview

"Zombie Showdown" is an engaging hyper-casual game where players must strategically battle waves of material-based zombies using a slingshot. The game challenges players to exploit the specific vulnerabilities of each zombie type—Fire, Ice, Wood—by selecting the most effective projectile.

My Role

I worked as a Game Developer in a team of four, contributing to the programming and implementation of core gameplay mechanics, including the unique interaction system where players target zombie weaknesses with specific projectiles.

Gameplay Highlights

  • Strategic Combat: Players must choose the right projectile to counter each zombie type, adding a layer of strategy to the fast-paced gameplay.
  • Material-Based Zombies: Different zombie types have distinct weaknesses—Fire, Ice, and Wood—that players must exploit to progress through the game.
  • Engaging Mechanics: The slingshot mechanic provides intuitive controls, making the game accessible while still offering depth and challenge.

Team Collaboration

This project was a collaborative effort, with each team member bringing their unique skills to the table. My contributions focused on gameplay development, ensuring that the interactions and mechanics were both fun and functional.
